About SmartHire

Connecting talented candidates with the right opportunities.

Who We Are

SmartHire is a role-based job portal developed using Flask, MySQL, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and JavaScript. The platform connects job seekers with recruiters while giving administrators complete control over job postings, applications and companies.

Our Mission

To simplify the hiring process by providing one platform where recruiters can post opportunities, candidates can apply easily, and administrators can manage the recruitment workflow efficiently.

Platform Highlights

  • ✅ Role-Based Authentication
  • ✅ Candidate Registration & Login
  • ✅ Recruiter Job Submission
  • ✅ Admin Approval Workflow
  • ✅ Resume Upload
  • ✅ Search & Filter Jobs
  • ✅ Application Tracking
